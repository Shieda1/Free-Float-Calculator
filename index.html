What is the free float calculator?
The free float calculator is a tool that calculates the number of free float shares a company has.
How to calculate free float percentage?
To calculate free float percentage you should use the following formula:
free float percentage = ((outstanding shares - restricted shares - closely held shares) / outstanding shares) * 100
Why use free float calculator?
The free float calculator is used in order to determine the number of free float shares a company has.
